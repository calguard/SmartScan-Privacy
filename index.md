# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

## 1. Introduction

SmartScan ("we," "our," or "us") is a document scanner app that helps you digitize and organize your documents. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Camera & Documents
- SmartScan uses your device camera to scan documents
- Scans are processed locally on your device
- We do not upload your documents to our servers unless you choose to sync with Google Drive

### Google Drive Sync (Optional)
- When you sign in with Google, we can sync your scans to your Google Drive
- Files are stored in a "SmartScan" folder in your Google Drive
- You can disconnect at any time from Settings

### Local Storage
- All scans are stored locally on your device by default
- You can delete any scan at any time

## 3. How We Use Information

- **Provide the app**: Process and display scanned documents
- **Sync to Drive**: When enabled, upload scans to your Google Drive
- **Improve the app**: Analyze usage patterns to improve functionality

## 4. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your personal information with third parties except:
- **Google Drive**: When you choose to sync, files are stored on your Google Drive
- **Legal requirements**: If required by law

## 5. Your Rights

- Access your data at any time
- Delete your data (local files or Google Drive files)
- Disconnect Google Drive sync in Settings

## 6. Contact

For questions about this policy, contact us through the app's Help section.

---

*This policy applies to SmartScan version 1.0 and later.*
